      Two GIS graphs show Kyoto Prefecture and Tokyo Prefecture in orange. I used the red color for Preservation Districts for Groups of Traditional Buildings. I also used blue color for Priority Districts for the Historic Landscape Maintenance and Improvement Plan. The southeast of Kyoto has blue. The center and north part of Kyoto have red. Nevertheless, Tokyo does not have any color on orange, which means there are no such preservation districts in Tokyo.
