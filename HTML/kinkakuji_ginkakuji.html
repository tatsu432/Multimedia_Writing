      Kinkaku-Ji (Gold Temple) and Ginkaku-Ji (Silver Temple) are in Kyoto. Both of them are temples of the Shokoku-Ji religious corporation. However, except for that, there are many differences between these two temples. Ginkaku-Ji expresses the emperor's authority while Ginkaku-Ji shows frugality. Kinkaku-Ji is very famous for its gorgeousness with its top Chinese phoenix. Ginkaku-Ji played a significant role in establishing the fundamental style of the place where the Japanese play Sado.
