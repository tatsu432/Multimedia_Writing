the shape of Kendama

Source: Tatsuhiro Shimizu. (2021). the shape of Kendama.

      This GIF shows the typical Kendama authorized by Japan Kendama Association. The red ball on the top of Kendama is called Tama (Ball). The Ken (grip) is made of wood. It has three Sara (cups) and one Kensaki (spike). The Ken and Tama are connected by a string.
