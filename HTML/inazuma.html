Inazuma Otoshi Ikkaiten Tōdai (Lightning drop 1-turn lighthouse)


Source: Tatsuhiro Shimizu. (2021). Inazuma Otoshi Ikkaiten Tōdai (Lightning drop 1-turn lighthouse)

      Inazuma Otoshi Ikkaiten Tōdai (Lightning drop 1-turn lighthouse) is a trick mainly for the young. In Lightning drop 1-turn lighthouse, the player firstly holds the sword of Kendama. After throwing Kendama into the air, the player waits until the Kendama rotates twice. Then, the player grabs the ball and gets the sword on the ball. Since this trick is speedy, it might be a trick for the young.
