Source: Tatsuhiro Shimizu. (2021). Furiken (Swing In) and Tōdai (Lighthouse).

      Furiken (Swing In) and Tōdai (Lighthouse) are tricks mainly for the elderly. In Swing In, the player rotates the ball once after that, the ball falls into the sword. In Lighthouse, the player holds the ball and pulls the Kendama up to get the sword on the ball. Both tricks are not so fast that these tricks would be suitable for the elderly. You can also play Tōdai (Lighthouse) by animation here.
